To answer the Think About It question: Why is it important to clear the container before adding new elements? What would happen if you skipped this step? It is important to clear the container, so duplicate items don't accidentally get printed between reloads, and to make sure the information is updated properly. If the container wasn't cleared and the user reloaded the site, content may not update properly, or if it did, append in addition to what was already there as a duplicate.
